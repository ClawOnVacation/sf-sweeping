About This Site

Don't Get Swept is a free SF street sweeping reminder service. Enter your address, see your sweeping schedule, and sign up for reminders 1 hour before cleaning starts.


Schedule data is sourced from SF Public Works published routes. Notifications are opt-in and you can unsubscribe at any time via link in any reminder.


SMS and email notifications are coming soon — sign up now and we'll activate them for your address automatically.
